Ro and Wenny's Sushi Palace

Where You Taste The Heaven of Sushi

Located in Forest Hills Garden in Queens, New York, Ro and Wenny's Sushi Palace has served authentic Japanese food to the community since 2008. The owners create their own Japanese dishes in the traditional Kaiseki method with the high skilled technique and also serves Tokyo-Edomae style Sushi and Sashimi. Fresh fish from Japan arrive daily. Fine selection of Sake List and Craft Beer is also available. We serve a special Japanese dining experience to you.
